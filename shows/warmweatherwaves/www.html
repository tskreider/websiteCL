About the Venue

"Ft. Henry was created as a way to bring local musicians back together in the 757. We started this in order to see if the need for a local DIY venue was still around and found out soon after, it surely is. Ft. Henry at it’s core is a venue built with emphasis on new bands looking for a welcoming place to play. We’ve all been the new guys at some point and know how hard it can be at the beginning to find people who are willing to book someone with limited experience. If you know of anyone or are looking for a killer venue to play at while you’re in Norfolk let us know and we would love to work with you!" - Ft. Henry
