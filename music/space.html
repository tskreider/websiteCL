Space - 2018

Space is the second E.P released by Same Time Tomorrow and it is truly "out of this world". Hard hitting riffs with fun choruses and deep lyrical content; the follow up release blew trash out of the water. Space focuses on distance. Whether it's emotionally, or literally with long distance relationships. The E.P was recorded by the band but mixed and mastered by Will Beasley.
