Trash - 2016

Trash is a self recorded and produced E.P! It was released in 2016. Before this, the band had a female lead singer. After the departure, things turned sour. Tensions were high and the band page was flooded with negative comments saying how we would be terrible without her, garbage at best. This partially inspired the name. We also knew looking back on it that it would probably sound terrible in comparison to what we would release in the future. We were right.
