About the Venue

Riffhouse Pub

One of the hottest bars south of Norfolk. A Place for bands, good times, and some great food! Large private parking lot , smoking bar and a non smoking bar on band side . Featuring national bands to local bands, Riffhouse has been a big part in holding down the music scene in the 757. Come out for some sick tunes and tasty food!
